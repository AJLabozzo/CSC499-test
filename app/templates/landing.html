Welome to the Southern Sustainability App!

Our goal is to develop a web application that serves as a tool for promoting the sustainability community within SCSU. We want to give students the ability to learn about sustainability projects and opportunities on campus while incentivizing involvement. To do this, we are creating a badging system utilizing the 17 Sustainability Goals proposed by the United Nations within the application. Each user will have their own profile page showcasing projects they’re involved in and badges earned. They will be able to submit and edit their projects, as well as intuitively search site information by filtering the Sustainable Goals to their needs or interests.

What are the Sustainable Development Goals?
"The Sustainable Development Goals are a call for action by all countries – poor, rich and middle-income – to promote prosperity while protecting the planet. They recognize that ending poverty must go hand-in-hand with strategies that build economic growth and address a range of social needs including education, health, social protection, and job opportunities, while tackling climate change and environmental protection." -United Nations SDG

Here's how to get involved:

  1. Feature your own work by creating an account
  2. Click on a Goal in the Goals page to find Projects, Research, Clubs, Southern Involvement, Operations, & Courses/Ciriculum
  3. Go to Opportunies to find Events, Internships, & Positions
